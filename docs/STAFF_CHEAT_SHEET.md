# Staff Cheat Sheet

One page. Keep this taped behind the bar.

## Logging in

- Tap your iPad to wake.
- Enter your 4 digit PIN.

## Taking an order

1. Tap drinks from the grid - they add to the cart on the right.
2. Confirm price with the customer. Prices in the cart are LIVE - they will update if the market moves.
3. Tap CHARGE when the customer is ready to pay. The price locks at that moment.
4. Card terminal handles the rest.
5. If they want a receipt, type email or phone into the receipt box before charging.

## Tabs (open order for a customer who'll order more)

1. Add the first round to the cart.
2. Tap "Save as tab". Type a name (e.g. "Sarah - bar 3"). Tick the "ID checked" box if you've verified photo ID.
3. The tab appears in the "Open tabs" row at the top of the POS.
4. When the customer comes back, tap their tab, add more drinks, then CHARGE.

## RSA refusal of service

If you refuse to serve anyone (intoxication, ID, behaviour), tap **RSA refuse** at the top of the POS, choose the reason, add a one-line note, and submit. This is logged for Liquor Licensing audits. Do this even if it feels obvious - it's our legal protection.

## Quantity shortcuts

- Tap a drink button = +1
- Right-click (or long-press) a drink button = type a quantity
- In the cart, you can type the exact quantity into the number field, or use +4 / +6 / +8 shortcut buttons for a round.

## Voiding

- Before charge: just tap the minus button on the cart line. No auth needed.
- After charge: tap "Manager void", enter the order id (shown in the toast), get a manager to enter their PIN.

## Crash

- When the screens go red and a stinger plays: a crash is on. The POS cart auto-prices to the discount. Just keep selling.
- If the cart was already filling before the crash started, the line prices update with a "MOVED" marker. Confirm with the customer before charging.

## Offline mode

- If the POS shows OFFLINE in amber, keep taking orders normally. Orders queue locally.
- Do NOT close the iPad or clear browser data. The queue lives in the browser.
- When the WiFi comes back, the orders auto-send. You'll see the queue counter drop.

## End of shift

- The senior staff member (manager or above) closes the shift in admin.
- Cash up your float against the Z-report (manager-only).

## House rules

- The 2 second tick is sacred. Do not refresh the display TVs unless they show a JavaScript error.
- The crash floor is 30% margin above cost. If a drink would breach this, the system holds the price at the floor automatically. This is not a bug.
- No em dashes anywhere. Just kidding (kind of).
